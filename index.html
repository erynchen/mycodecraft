About My Code Craft

We're a personalized coding education business based in Ajax, Ontario, dedicated to providing one-on-one instruction to children (ages 5-13). Our approach is based on comprehensive personalized assessment, tailored projects, and individual attention.

  • Customized learning plans based on individual assessment
  • Project-based curriculum using robotics and Python
  • One-on-one instruction focused on your specific needs
  • Flexible scheduling with sessions at Ajax community centers
  • Inclusive approach welcoming students of all ability levels

Our mission is to empower young minds through engaging, accessible, and personalized coding education. We offer affordable pricing at $45 per session.
